Healdsburg is a charming small town located in the heart of Sonoma County. Healdsburg's five miles are home to three of Sonoma’s major viticulture areas – Russian River, Alexander Valley, and Dry Creek. There are plenty of hotel and house rental options within walking distance to all of the weekend's events. Book fast as most of the hotels are small and fill up quickly.
