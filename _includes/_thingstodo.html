Healdsburg has many options for restaurants, wineries, and things to do. Here are some of our favorites.

The Shed restaurant

Shed Cafe brings together all of the elements we believe in: responsible farming, ingredient-driven cooking, and flavorful food. Perfect for a sit down lunch or to grab items for a picnic.

This dynamic contemporary American in Healdsburg’s Hotel Les Mars impresses with a terrific small-plates selection inspired by the seasons and crafted from just-picked ingredients.

http://chalkboardhealdsburg.com

This rrelaxed, all-day Healdsburg destination from the Scopa team turns out outstanding wood-oven-fired pizzas and creative Italian small plates.
